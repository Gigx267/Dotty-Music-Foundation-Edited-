How it Started

Founded by Derek Odaro (Dotty), who was born in Birmingham, United Kingdom, the Dotty Music Foundation was established to bridge the gap between raw talent and professional opportunity.

Having witnessed the immense creative potential in Nigerian music, fashion, and dance, Dotty used his international perspective to create a movement that provides recording deals, professional video shoots, and mentorship to those who need it most.

Our impact is real: in November 2023, we empowered SOUNDBOI as the winner of our youth empowerment program, funding his professional music promotion and official video shoot to launch his career to the next level.
