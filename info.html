The Dotty Music Foundation is an Abuja-based organization focused on mentoring and empowering talented, underprivileged youth to pursue careers in the music industry. It is led by Derek Odaro, who serves as the President and CEO.The foundation is primarily known for its talent development initiatives and financial support for emerging artists. Here are some key details about their work:Youth Empowerment Through Music: This is the foundation's flagship program, designed to discover and mentor unsigned talent across various creative fields, including songwriters, music producers, guitarists, DJs, video directors, and dancers.Talent Hunt Competitions: In late 2023, the foundation held a major talent hunt in Abuja with a grand finale at the Transcorp Hilton. The initiative featured:A N1 million cash prize for the winner.Recording contracts and management deals for the top finalists.Distribution of musical equipment to runners-up.Mentorship: The foundation leverages industry veterans to guide participants. Past resource persons and mentors mentioned in connection with their events include figures like Shina Rambo and Joel Amadi.Mission: Their core goal is to provide a platform for those who lack access to resources, viewing the music industry as a viable path for youth employment and a way to reduce crime by channeling creative energy into professional opportunitiesThe foundation operates in a space that is becoming increasingly structured. For instance, while Dotty Music Foundation focuses on Abuja-based talent hunts and direct financial grants, other initiatives like the recently launched Tiwa Savage Music Foundation (March 2026) are focusing on specialized training in partnership with institutions like Berklee College of Music.
